Why CHOCCO?

Our Swiss-made chocolate isn't just dark, deep and unbelievably smooth – it's also crafted from organic cacao by farmers who are replanting South American rainforests.

  • Healthy

    No artificial flavors, emulsifiers, GMO and soy

  • Tasty

    Expert Swiss conching creates the smoothest chocolate in the world, ensuring you don't miss the sugar.

  • Nutritious

    Heirloom cows contribute their lush grass-fed milk to be churned, browned, and stirred into the malty depths of our signature Ecuadorian cacao.
